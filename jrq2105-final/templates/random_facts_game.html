Two Truths and a Lie

Two truths and a lie game with random facts about me (accurate as of the time I'm writing this). For each set of three statements, two of them are true and one of them is a lie (except for the bonus question). See if you guess the lies correctly by clicking the "reveal lie" buttons.

A: I've eaten a whole ghost pepper before.

B: I've eaten escargot before.

C: I've eaten haggis before.

A: I've never been stung by a bee.

B: I've never gotten food poisoning.

C: I've never broken a bone.

A: I've been pooped on by a bird.

B: I am trilingual.

C: I think the food at Taco Bell tastes good.

BONUS: This time, there are two truths and two lies, and these are not random facts about me. Which statement MUST be a lie?.

A: Statements B and C are either a true statement and a lie, respectively, or a lie and a true statement, respectively.

B: Statement A and Statement C are both true statements if this statement is a lie.

C: This is a true statement.

D: This statement and Statement B are either both true statements or both lies.
